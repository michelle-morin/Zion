Fun Facts About Zion National Park

  • Zion National Park was founded November 19, 1919
  • The nearest city to Zion National Park is Springdale, Utah.
  • Zion National Park is distinguished by steep red cliffs.

Zion has three campgrounds.

  1. The Lava Point Campground (about an hour drive from Zion canyon)
  2. South Campground
  3. Watchman Campground

Recommended hikes:

  • The Watchman
    • Easy, 3-mile roundtrip
    • Less than 400' of climbing
  • The Narrows
    • Easy to strenuous hike upstream in the Virgin River
    • Variable distance, up to 16 miles
  • Canyon Overlook Trail
    • Easy, 1 mile roundtrip
    • A favorite among photographers!
  • Angel's Landing via West Rim Trail
    • Strenuous, 5-mile roundtrip
    • Not for those fearful of heights!
